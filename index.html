Liuke Yang

Liuke is a product manager at Super.com, a Series C startup empowering users to spend less, save more, and build credit. To date, Super.com has helped 7M customers save over $150M.


Prior, Liuke was a product manager at Extra, a Series B fintech unicorn. At Extra, he worked alongside the co-founder to reinvent the credit building journey for over 200K members. Before joining Extra, he was an investment analyst at REB Capital Management where he focused on emerging markets fintech research. He earned a Bachelor of Arts in economics from Vanderbilt University.


Liuke lives in Dallas with his wife and three dachshunds.
